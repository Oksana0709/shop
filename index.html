An Endless Pursuit of Nature

In 1853 Ole Andreas Devold embarked on his
journey, and since then our wool has been formed in
the roughest conditions. Today, we build on our
tradition of mastering wool. Relying on our
dedication to craftmanship, we develop the finest
quality from the most robust material. Crafted to
withstand storms and to comfort on late summer
nights. With profound care for nature, we create
knitwear that lasts, that can be passed on – a legacy
for times to come.
